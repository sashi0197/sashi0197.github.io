Student Tribe

St-33, Batch-1

Student Tribe, founded by Sri Charan Lakkaraju, is not just a platform or an app but is a movement that empowers futures through networking. With a resolute focus on Tier 2 and Tier 3 colleges, Charan's idea is simple yet revolutionary: Talent and skill triumph over the prestige of one's alma mater.
